These rankings were decided by repeated simulations of 2 way pot equity - higher equity indicates higher rank. First, 100% of the range was simulated against each other, and the bottom 50% of hands there were given their ranks. Then the top 50% were run with each other, without the bottom 50%. This was repeated for the brackets of 30%, 20%, 10% and 5% , which means the better hands have a more accurate ranking.
