短牌游戏简介

短牌德州是德州扑克的一个变种，我们将一副牌里面的所以2-5的牌移除，使一副牌从52张牌变为36张。 因为牌的改变，短牌德州扑克的规则对比常规德州有一些改变。 牌力大小顺序如下：

三条 > 顺子:

  1. 高牌
  2. 一对
  3. 两队
  4. 顺子
  5. 三条
  6. 葫芦
  7. 同花
  8. 四条
  9. 同花顺
  10. 皇家同花顺

顺子 > 三条:

  1. 高牌
  2. 一对
  3. 两队
  4. 三条
  5. 顺子
  6. 葫芦
  7. 同花
  8. 四条
  9. 同花顺
  10. 皇家同花顺

Introduction to shorty solver

For this tutorial, we will assume you know the rules of standard Texas Holdem poker.

Game Intro

Short deck poker is a new variant of Texas Holdem, it removes the 2-5 from each suit, make the deck 36 cards instead of 52. Since the number of cards changes, the rules of the game are slightly different with standard Texas holdem. The hand rankings we are using are the following:

Three of a kind > Straight:

  1. High Card
  2. One Pair
  3. Two Pairs
  4. Straight
  5. Three of a kind
  6. Full House
  7. Flush
  8. Four of a kind
  9. Straight Flush
  10. Royal Flush

Straight > Three of a kind:

  1. High Card
  2. One Pair
  3. Two Pairs
  4. Three of a kind
  5. Straight
  6. Full House
  7. Flush
  8. Four of a kind
  9. Straight Flush
  10. Royal Flush

{% if tripsFlag %} The current ruleset is three of a kind > straight. You can change this on the top-right of the page at any time. {% else %} The current ruleset is straight > three of a kind. You can change this on the top-right of the page at any time. {% endif %}

Also, since 2-5 are removed from the deck, now Ace can also be used as a 5 for straight. Thus, A,6,7,8,9 is also a straight.
