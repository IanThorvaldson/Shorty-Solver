短牌破解器教学

软件功能

变更游戏规则

您可以使用右上角的按钮来改变当前短牌游戏规则，可以在顺子大于三条和三条大于顺子两种规则中切换。按钮的当前状态就是当前软件所使用的游戏规则。

手牌胜率计算器

更定最多6位玩家的精确手牌（可以提供最多5张公共牌牌面），我们可以计算每位玩家的当前胜率。

范围胜率计算器

给定每位玩家一定范围的手牌，我们计算出当前范围之间的胜率。

翻牌前范围构建

从手牌矩阵里选择手牌，可以模拟出选择的手牌占总手牌的百分比，并计算出对战前百分之5%，10%，20%，50%，100%的范围胜率。

手牌翻牌胜率分布

此功能帮助您了解特定手牌在对战对手手牌范围时，在翻牌的胜率表现分布。

给与玩家一个精确手牌，给与对手一个范围，点击模拟，一个柱状分布图将展示您的手牌在对战对手范围时的翻牌胜率分布。

输入语法

卡牌输入语法：我们使用6，7，8，9，T, J, Q, K, A这些大写字母和数字来表达从6到Ace的卡牌。同时我们用小写字母c,d,h,s来分别表达草花(clubs)，方片(diamonds)，红桃(hearts)和黑桃(spades)这些花色。举例，红桃Ace就是Ah，方片8就是8d。它们在一起作为手牌就被表示为Ah8d

范围输入语法：当使用范围作为表达时，我们需要支持各种组合的存在。比如JT，表示着所有Jack-十的组合。 同时如果我们想区分表达同色和不同色的组合，我们需要用小写s表达同色组合，小写o表达不同色组合。比如JTs和JTo。我们使用逗号”,”来区分组合，比如如果我想表达Ace和King红桃和所有Jack十的组合我们用如下的表达方式：

AhKh,JT

对于范围，您也可以选择使用百分比来表达前x%的手牌，具体的手牌排名定义请在手牌牌面的界面查看。比如，如果您想输入前5%的手牌和口袋对6作为范围，那么您需要输入

5%,66

Tutorial of ShortySolver Software

Software features

Changing ruleset

You may change the ruleset at any time by using the button on the top-right of any page. This will also display the current ruleset.

Poker Hand odds calculator

Given player specific hands (up to 6 players) with or without community card information (0-5 board cards), and we find out the winning odds of each player. Each player has exact two cards in their hand

Range calculator

Given different sets of possible poker hands, find out the winning equities of each set (ranges).

Preflop range constructor

Select the preflop range you want from the hands matrix, and calculate to find heads up equities vs. top 5%, 10%, 20%, 50% and 100% of hands.

Flop Equity Distribution

This feature helps you to understand how often a hand has certain equity on the flop against a range of hands in heads-up pots.

Giving a specific hand for player1 and provide a range of hands for opponents, you will get a bar chart which shows often your hand has certain equity on the flop.

Input Syntax

Card syntax: we use 6,7,8,9,T,J,Q,K,A to represent from 6 to Ace, and lower case letter c,d,h,s to represent suits for clubs,diamonds,hearts and spades. For example, Ace of heart would be Ah, 8 of diamond would be 8d. These together in a hand is Ah8d.

Range syntax: for range calculation, we also support combos, so syntax like JT for all combos of Jack Ten is accepted. Also, we can do JTs for all Jack-Ten suited, JTo for all Jack Ten offsuit. We use ',' to separate combos, so for Ace King of hearts and all JT combos, we do:

AhKh,JT

For range, you may also do a top percentage of hands, as defined in "Hand Rankings". This is done in the range calculator by simply putting x%, where x is the top percentage of hands you want to consider. So, for example, if we want the top 5% of hands and any pair of 6's:

5%,66
